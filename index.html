Who We Are

We are small local travel agency in Jaisalmer which was founded by Vinay Singh. We specialize in camel safari packages, and we are proudly run and managed by the villagers from the desert.

Learn More

Why Choose Us

We follow the traditional phrase ‘Padharo Mhare Desh’, which means that you are welcome to our country/village. This not only stands as our welcome message but also reveals the hospitality of the people of Rajasthan. We at Trotters, respect our guests and welcome you to be a part of the rich culture and tradition of Jaisalmer. We consider all our guests as extensions of our family, and we look forward to providing you with delightful camel safari experience like no other in Jaisalmer.

Learn More

Community Involvement

Being a local of the Thar Desert, we understand the hardships faced by the people in the desert, so the idea of founding Trotters was to provide these people with a good normal lifestyle that they deserve. Hence, we are powered by the people from the Thar Desert.

Our Safari Packages

We'll arrange everything for you to ensure that you enjoy your camel safari.

Half-Day Sunrise Camel Safari
starts at ₹ 1250 / person per day Includes meals.

The Half-Day Sunrise Camel Safari is a perfect getaway for those who wish to experience the desert life in a short span of time. This safari will offer you the freedom to enjoy the beautiful sunrise in the desert along with having a delicious breakfast with your family and friends. Enjoy and click amazing pictures as the desert glitters with the warmth of the sun as it breaks out on the horizon, turning the sky red. This tour is ideal for those who want to explore the desert and also want to save their time and visit the beautiful city of Jaisalmer later in the day.

starts at ₹ 1250 / person per day Includes meals.

The Half-Day Sunset Camel Safari is a perfect getaway for those who wish to experience the desert life in a short span of time. This safari will offer you the freedom to enjoy the beautiful sunset in the desert and spend some quality time with your friends and family, watching the sun dip in behind the mighty dunes. You can mount on your camels and feel like a explorer in the desert while our guides prepare delicious and mouthwatering dinner, especially for your indulgence. You can also cherish the beauties of the mesmerizing night sky on your ride to Jaisalmer, in a jeep.

One-Day Camel Safari
starts at ₹ 1250 / person per day Includes meals.

The One-Day Camel Safari heads straight towards the sunrise point by a jeep, introducing you to our friendly guides and your clean & healthy camels. Enjoy the beautiful sunrise in the desert followed by a delicious breakfast. Afterwards, mount on your camels and ride for a couple of hours into the desert. You may rest under the shade for a long lunch break. Next, we proceed towards the sunset point. Enjoy the surroundings as the sun dips behind the sand while our guides prepare delicious dinner for you. After dinner, leave for Jaisalmer by a jeep.
